Sparsifies a tensor according to the provided SparseMap.

The most general SparseMap is an element-to-element map. Given such a map, and the tilings of the tensors, we can make any other sparse map. This overload of from_sparse_map is at the moment the workhorse for all of the overloads.

Template Parameters
TThe type of the tensor being sparsified, assumed to be a TiledArray tensor.
Parameters
[in]esmThe element-to-element sparse map describing how to sparsify the tensor.
[in]tensorThe tensor we are sparsifying.
[in]outer_trangeThe TiledRange for the outer tensor of the tensor-of-tensor this function is creating.
[in]ind2modeA map from independent index mode to the mode in tensor it maps to. i.e., ind2mode[i] is the mode of tensor that the i-th mode of an independent index maps to.
Returns
The tensor-of-tensors resulting from applying esm to tensor.

Wraps the creation of a sparse map from a pair of independent indices to the union of the dependent indices.

Given a sparse map , which goes from to , and a sparse map , which goes from to , this function will make a new sparse map which goes from to . Only pairs consistent with are made.

This function essentially does a direct product on the independent indices and a union on the dependent indices, where the direct product is sparse.

Template Parameters
IndIndexTypeThe type of the independent indices in both sparse maps. Expected to be either TileIndex or ElementIndex.
DepIndexTypeThe type of the dependent indices in L_ia. Expected to be either TileIndex or ElementIndex.
Parameters
[in]L_iaThe sparse map we are turning into a pair map.
[in]L_ijThe sparsity relationship among the independent indices of L_ia.
Returns
The sparse map resulting from the direct product of the independent indices in L_ia (subject to the sparsity relationship L_ij) and the union of the dependent indices.
Exceptions
std::out_of_rangeif an independent or dependent index of L_ij is not in L_ia. Strong throw guarantee.
std::runtime_errorif the indepdendent (or dependent) indices in L_ij are not of the same rank as the independent indices in L_ia. Strong throw guarantee.
std::runtime_errorif the independent rank of L_ia is not 1. Strong throw guarantee.

Reduces a tensor-of-tensors to a regular tensor by summing over "corresponding" elements.

Reduces the outer indices of a tensor-of-tensor by summing the elements. In einsum notation: A(j,k;) = B(i;j,k) The inner indices of B are now tiled indices in A.

Template Parameters
TThe type of the tensor being reduced, assumed to be a TiledArray tensor-of-tensors.
Parameters
[in]esmThe element-to-element sparse map which was used to sparsify the tensor.
[in]t_of_tThe tensor-of-tensors we are reducing.
[in]trange_rvThe TiledRange for the output tensor (tiles the inner indices of t_of_t).
Returns
The tensor resulting from applying esm to tensor.
