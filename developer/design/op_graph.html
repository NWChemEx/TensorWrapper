Designing the OpGraph

OpGraph Considerations

Multiple source nodes

The graph may have multiple inputs. Each input is assumed to be a TensorWrapper object.

Multiple sink nodes

The graph may have multiple results. We assume the results have different types based on whether or not they are terminating the graph. The graph terminates on a TensorWrapper object.
